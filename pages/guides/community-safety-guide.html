What is Community Safety Network?

Rakshita's Community Safety Network connects travelers, locals, and safety ambassadors to share real-time safety information, tips, and assistance. The community-driven approach ensures you have access to the most current safety insights from people who know the area best.

Community Features

Real-Time Updates

Share and receive instant safety updates about incidents, road conditions, and area-specific safety tips from community members.

Safety Ambassadors

Connect with verified local safety ambassadors who provide guidance, assistance, and local knowledge to help you stay safe.

Area Reviews

Read and write detailed safety reviews of locations, including lighting, crowd levels, and overall safety impressions.

Mutual Assistance

Request help from nearby community members or offer assistance to fellow travelers in need of guidance or support.

How to Use Community Features

1

Join the Community

Create your community profile and set your preferences for receiving updates. Choose to be anonymous or share your identity with verified safety ambassadors.

2

Share Safety Updates

Report incidents, share safety tips, or post area reviews to help other travelers. Your contributions help build a comprehensive safety database for everyone.

3

Receive Real-Time Alerts

Get instant notifications about safety updates in your area or planned route. Community members share real-time information about incidents, closures, and safety concerns.

4

Connect with Ambassadors

Reach out to local safety ambassadors for personalized advice, assistance, or emergency support. Ambassadors are verified community members with local expertise.
