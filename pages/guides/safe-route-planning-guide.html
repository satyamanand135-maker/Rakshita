What is Safe Route Planning?

Rakshita's Safe Route Planning system goes beyond traditional navigation by prioritizing your safety. Simply enter place names like "India Gate" or "Airport" and get multiple route options with detailed safety analysis, real-time threat assessment, and interactive map visualization.

Route Options Available

Safest Route (95% Safe)

Prioritizes safety over speed with well-lit streets, high police presence, CCTV coverage, and low crime areas. May take 15% longer but ensures maximum security.

Balanced Route (85% Safe)

Perfect balance of safety and efficiency with mix of main and side roads, moderate lighting, and regular traffic flow. Good compromise for most situations.

Fastest Route (70% Safe)

Direct path with minimal delays but includes safety warnings for poorly lit sections. Not recommended after dark or in unfamiliar areas.

How to Plan Safe Routes

1

Enter Place Names

Simply type familiar place names like "India Gate", "Airport", "Railway Station", or "Hospital". No need for coordinates or complex addresses - the system understands natural language input.

2

AI Safety Analysis

The system analyzes multiple safety factors including lighting conditions, police presence, crime statistics, CCTV coverage, and real-time threat data to calculate route safety scores.

3

Choose Your Route

Review multiple route options with detailed safety information, estimated travel time, and specific features. Each route shows safety score, warnings, and recommendations.

4

Interactive Navigation

Start turn-by-turn navigation with real-time safety updates, emergency SOS access, and the ability to switch routes if conditions change during your journey.
