What is Real-Time Location Tracking?

Rakshita's location tracking system uses advanced GPS technology to monitor your precise location in real-time. It automatically shares your location with trusted contacts and emergency services when needed, ensuring help can find you quickly in any situation.

How to Use Location Tracking

1

Enable Location Access

When you first open Rakshita, your browser will ask for location permission. Click "Allow" to enable precise GPS tracking.

2

View Your Current Location

Your current location will be displayed in the dashboard with coordinates and a readable address. The system automatically updates every 30 seconds.

3

Set Up Trusted Contacts

Add family members and friends who should receive your location updates. They'll be notified automatically in emergency situations.

4

Monitor Location Accuracy

Check the GPS accuracy indicator to ensure optimal tracking. Green means excellent accuracy (¬±10m), yellow means good (¬±50m).
