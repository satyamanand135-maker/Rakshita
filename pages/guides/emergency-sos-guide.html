What is Emergency SOS?

Rakshita's Emergency SOS system provides instant access to emergency services with a single button press. It automatically calls emergency numbers, shares your precise location, and notifies your trusted contacts. The system works on both mobile devices and laptops with different optimized approaches.

How It Works on Different Devices

Mobile Devices

  • Automatically opens phone dialer
  • Calls 112, 100, 108, 101 in sequence
  • Calls your personal emergency contacts
  • Shares GPS location via SMS
  • Works with all smartphones

Laptops & Desktops

  • Displays emergency numbers clearly
  • Copy-to-clipboard functionality
  • Shows your location coordinates
  • Instructions for manual dialing
  • Contact management interface

How to Use Emergency SOS

1

Press the SOS Button

Find the red Emergency SOS button in the app or dashboard. Press it once to activate the emergency protocol. The button is designed to be easily accessible in stressful situations.

2

5-Second Countdown

A 5-second countdown begins to prevent accidental activation. You can cancel during this time if pressed by mistake. The countdown shows your location being captured.

3

Automatic Emergency Calls

After countdown, the system automatically calls emergency services. On mobile, it opens your dialer. On desktop, it shows numbers to call manually on your phone.

4

Location & Contact Alerts

Your precise GPS location is automatically shared with emergency services and your trusted contacts are notified with your location and emergency status.
