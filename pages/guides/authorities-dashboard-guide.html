What is the Authorities Dashboard?

The Rakshita Authorities Dashboard is a comprehensive command center designed for law enforcement, emergency services, and government agencies. It provides real-time monitoring of tourist movements, predictive analytics for incident prevention, and instant emergency response coordination with precise location data and threat assessment capabilities.

Key Dashboard Features

Live Monitoring

Real-time tracking of tourist movements, density maps, and location analytics with privacy-compliant aggregated data visualization.

Emergency Response

Instant SOS alerts with precise GPS coordinates, user profiles, and automated dispatch recommendations for rapid response.

Predictive Analytics

AI-driven insights for incident prediction, resource allocation, and proactive safety measure deployment based on patterns.

Command Center

Centralized control panel for managing safety zones, coordinating multi-agency responses, and maintaining situational awareness.

How to Use the Authorities Dashboard

1

Access & Authentication

Secure login with multi-factor authentication for authorized personnel. Role-based access ensures officers, dispatchers, and administrators see relevant information for their responsibilities.

2

Real-Time Monitoring

View live tourist density maps, movement patterns, and safety zone status. Monitor high-traffic areas, identify potential crowd issues, and track safety metrics across your jurisdiction.

3

Emergency Response

Receive instant SOS alerts with caller location, profile information, and recommended response units. Coordinate with medical services, dispatch officers, and track response times.

4

Analytics & Reporting

Generate comprehensive reports on incident patterns, response effectiveness, and safety trends. Use predictive analytics to allocate resources and prevent incidents before they occur.
