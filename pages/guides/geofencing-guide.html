What is Geo-Fencing?

Geo-fencing creates virtual safety boundaries around locations using GPS technology. Rakshita's AI-powered system automatically detects when you enter or exit different safety zones and sends appropriate alerts to keep you informed about your surroundings.

Safety Zone Types

Safe Zones

Well-lit areas with high police presence, CCTV coverage, and low crime rates. You'll receive confirmation when entering these secure areas.

Caution Zones

Areas requiring extra attention due to moderate risk factors. You'll get alerts with safety tips and recommendations.

High-Risk Zones

Areas with elevated safety concerns. Immediate alerts with alternative route suggestions and emergency contact notifications.

How Geo-Fencing Works

1

Automatic Zone Detection

As you move around, Rakshita's AI continuously analyzes your location against our safety database. No manual setup required - the system works automatically.

2

Real-Time Zone Alerts

When you enter a new safety zone, you'll receive instant notifications with relevant information about the area's safety level and any precautions to take.

3

Smart Recommendations

Based on the zone type, receive personalized safety recommendations like staying on main roads, avoiding certain times, or contacting local authorities.

4

Emergency Contact Alerts

In high-risk zones, your trusted contacts are automatically notified of your location with options to check in or request immediate assistance.
